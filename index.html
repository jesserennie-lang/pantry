Welcome to Pantry.
Pantry. is YNAB for groceries. Track prices, plan hauls across multiple stores, and let AI help your budget go further. Let's get your household set up — takes about a minute.
Your household
You're the household admin. Name your household and tell us who's doing the shopping.
Invite family
Add family members who shop or plan with you. They'll share your list, pantry, and budget. You can always do this later.
Note: invites are saved to your household now. Sending real invite links arrives with accounts in a future update.
Where you shop
Pick the stores you shop at. These show up when you log hauls and items. You can add more anytime.
Monthly budget
Set your grocery budget for the month. The YNAB way: surplus isn't permission to spend — it builds your pantry buffer so you're always a little ahead.
How Pantry. works
